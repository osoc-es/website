Snipstory

Snipstory is a web application that is going to be used in primary schools all over Flanders. With Snipstory, children between the ages of 8 and 12 can discover different parts of history in a fun and interactive way.

Snipstory uses the life stories of normal people who lived through extraordinary times to make the big events easier to understand. After discovering these life stories, the children get to solve questions and eventually creatively engage with the content.

Other users of the web application are teachers who can log on to Snipstory and valuable content partners, such as museums, who can add stories to Snipstory.
