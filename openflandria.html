Open Flandria

Open Flandria is a website where you can access a digital gallery with open source images about sustainable houses and living. The user of the website will be able to download images without creating an account. However, for the registered users it will be possible to save the pictures in the custom galleries. This digital gallery is meant to be used to bring awareness to this topic and for educational purposes, and in the future become a reference for everyone who communicates about sustainable building and living in Flanders.
