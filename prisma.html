Prisma

This year, Prisma joined oSoc for a second time. Prisma is a web application that aims to bring care home staff and care home residents closer together through stories of their past. The application allows the family of care home residents to upload stories, pictures and videos that give the care home staff an idea of who this resident is. This way the care home staff can get to know the person and offer them a more personalized care.

The #oSoc18 team is working on a couple of functionalities in the app: they’re making it possible for families to invite each other and help each other tell the story of their family member.
